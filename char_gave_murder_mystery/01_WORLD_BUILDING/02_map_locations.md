## DETAILED LOCATION PROFILES

### वाडा (WADA) - The Manor

**Physical Description:**
- Large traditional mansion, two stories
- Once grand, now visibly declining
- Wooden beams showing rot
- Courtyard with dried tulsi plant
- Servant quarters attached
- Surrounding fields (mostly leased out now)

**Who Lives Here:**
- Gadkari Saheb (Zamindar) - 72
- Vasundhara (his wife) - 68
- Nalini (daughter, widowed) - 50
- Raghoba (old servant) - 70
- Haribhau (cook) - 55
- Kisan (young servant) - 40
- Arjun (grandson) - 28 [arriving]
- Priya (granddaughter) - 25 [in Pune]

**Key Scenes Here:**
- Arjun's arrival and recognition
- Leena treating Gadkari Saheb
- Raghoba's confession (Phase 4)
- Vasundhara's confrontation (Phase 4)

**Atmosphere:**
- Faded grandeur
- Secrets in every room
- Servants know more than masters
- Guilt permeates the walls

### राखे (RAKHE) - The Ashes

**Physical Description:**

- Murky water, sometimes foaming
- Surrounded by scrubland
- Jamun trees once lined it (now dead)

**Who Is Found Here:**
- Timeline 1: Ira & Asha (twins)
- Timeline 2: Neelam & Omkar Karande
- Timeline 3: Gauri More (Aarav escaped)

**Atmosphere:**
- Dread central
- Villagers avoid it
- "Cursed" ground
- Where ghosts are "seen"
