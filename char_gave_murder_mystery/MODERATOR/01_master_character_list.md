## SPEAKING PATTERNS

### Upper Caste/Authority (Formal Marathi):
- Zamindar, Vasundhara, Devrao Gokhale
- Use respectful forms, longer sentences
- Reference tradition, propriety

### Middle Class (Standard):
- Madhav, Sarpanch, Constable, Teacher
- Mix of formal and casual
- Business-like

### Working Class (Colloquial):
- Kiln workers, servants, Vasti residents
- Shorter sentences, direct
- More emotional expression

### Traumatized (Fragmented):
- Aarav, Kalyani, Rangubai
- Incomplete sentences, pauses
- Non-verbal communication

### Evasive (Careful):
- Raghoba, Madhav (when lying), Prakash
- Deflection, changing subjects
- Rehearsed responses
