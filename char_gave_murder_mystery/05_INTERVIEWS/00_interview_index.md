## INTERVIEW WRITING GUIDELINES

### Language:
- Simple, clear Marathi-influenced English
- Short sentences
- Rural speech patterns
- Period-appropriate (2002-2003)

### Character Voice:
- Upper caste: More formal, longer sentences
- Workers: Direct, simple, emotional
- Traumatized: Fragmented, pauses
- Evasive: Deflection, changing subjects

### Information Density:
- Phase 1: Heavy superstition, few facts
- Phase 2: Mix of fear and facts
- Phase 3: More facts, less fear
- Phase 4: Direct confrontation, confessions

### Length:
- Phase 1: Medium (establish atmosphere)
- Phase 2: Longer (more to uncover)
- Phase 3: Focused (key revelations)
- Phase 4: Intense (confrontations)
