### SECTION IV - DESCRIPTION OF THE OCCURRENCE

The complainant Shri Gopal Mane appeared at this police station on morning of 16-07-1988 in distressed condition. He stated that his two daughters namely Ira d/o Gopal Mane (age 8 yrs) and Asha d/o Gopal Mane (age 8 yrs), twin sisters, had gone to play near the lake area in the afternoon of 15-07-1988.

The mother of the deceased, Smt. Kalyani w/o Gopal Mane, was present at residence during the afternoon hours. She states she was not well and was resting. The children left around 16:00 hrs for playing with other village children as was their regular habit.

When children did not return by evening meal time (approx 19:30 hrs), the mother sent word to father at kiln. Search party organized by villagers including:
- Shri Tukaram Patil (farmer)
- Shri Dagdu Shinde (farmer)
- Shri Raghoba Kale (servant, Gadkari Wada)
- Three other persons (names not taken)

Search continued through night with lanterns. Bodies discovered by Shri Raghoba Kale at 06:15 hrs on 16-07-1988 in the northern portion of Korde Tal, partially submerged in shallow water area. Both deceased were found together, approximately 15 meters from bank.

Monsoon rains had been heavy in preceding days. Lake level had risen suddenly due to flash flooding from hill runoff. Water which was previously at low level had covered the playing area.

### SECTION VI - PRELIMINARY OPINION

Based on inquest and circumstances:

1. Heavy monsoon rainfall caused sudden rise in lake water level
2. Children were playing in area which became submerged
3. Being of young age (8 years), could not escape flash flooding
4. Death appears accidental in nature
5. No foul play suspected at this time

**Recommendation:** Case to be registered as Accidental Death Report (ADR)
No further investigation warranted.

---
