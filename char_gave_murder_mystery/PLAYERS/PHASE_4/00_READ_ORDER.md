## How to Win

You have fully solved the mystery when you can answer:

**Timeline 1 (Twins, 15 years ago):**
- Who ordered the killing?
- Who carried it out?
- Why?

**Timeline 2-3 (Recent deaths):**
- Who is the primary killer?
- Who helped them?
- Why did the children have to die?

**The Final Revelation:**
- How are the two conspiracies connected?
- Who created the curse legend, and why?
